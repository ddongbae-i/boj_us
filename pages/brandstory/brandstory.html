Our Living Heritage

Beauty in Korean culture has always been inseparable from virtue, health, and harmony with nature. The Gyuhap Chongseo stands as testament to this philosophy—a sacred text that preserved not merely beauty formulations, but a profound way of living that honors both inner cultivation and outer expression.

Today, we are the guardians of this extraordinary legacy. Every formulation honors the wisdom of Lady Bingheo, every ritual connects us to centuries of Korean women who understood that true beauty emanates from the harmony between natural ingredients and mindful practice.

Cultural Heritage

Royal Beauty Traditions of Joseon

For over 500 years, the royal court of Joseon Dynasty preserved sacred beauty rituals that embodied the Korean philosophy of natural harmony and inner radiance.

These time-honored traditions represent not merely cosmetic practices, but a profound cultural heritage that celebrates the eternal pursuit of beauty through nature's wisdom.
